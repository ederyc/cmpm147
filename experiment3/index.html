Experiment 3 - Procedural Tile World

Description

This generator generates landscapes of dusk in a foresty area with water. The world is covered in a slight darkness as night begins to creep in. You don't know quite where you are or what you're doing there, but the cool breeze and sound of moving water calms you in this eery setting.

**Codes**: '_' = background,

'~' = water,

'.' = dirt path

**Life**: This generator shows signs of life through the glowing orbs which constantly flash, providing light and guidance in this dark evening. (using tiles from [Micro Tileset - Overworld & Dungeon](https://thkaspar.itch.io/micro-tileset-overworld-dungeon) by [thkaspar](https://thkaspar.itch.io/))

Technical

This experiment generates a procedurally created world using tiles, Perlin noise, and seeded randomness. The user can toggle between an overworld and a dungeon style.
